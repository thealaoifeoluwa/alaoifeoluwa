Wogpay

Accessiblity to fast payment system is crucial to any and every community, With wogpay we looked to streamline the digital payment service process while also addressing the looming problem of usability issues, pre-purchase anxiety and trust issues.

Role

UI/UX Designer

Tool

Figma, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop

Deliverable

Digital Consignment,  Bill payment and tracking solution integration

Duration

10 weeks

Team

Self Directed, with guidance from mentor

The Problem

Lack of payment options and trust are the major challenges an average african faced when making use of digital payment services.

Our Solution

Create a platform that allows for an easier payment options around user's routines, usability issues, pre-purchase anxiety and trust issues.

Digital Payment

We designed a banking app for your busy lifestyle, that gives you fast accessibility and various payment options, within and outside the country.

Quick Loan   & Saving System

Our Process

We started things off by interviewing two people we saw as specialists in the two fields we were super interested in before landing on our final concept, Banking system and Payment services. At the beginning of this project we were un-sure with what route we wanted to take and we used these interviews to gather basic insights in both fields which lead us to find something that combined both

Head of banking Operations Quote
“It’s about the brand, it’s about the post purchase experience. Everything that you connect the consumer with whether it’s digital or physical should be about that experience and that interaction with the consumer”
Payment service Quote
“The rise of Digital Era brings us to a cashless E-commerce world. The buying behavior of the customer has changed drastically over the past years and in the center of all Mobile payment gateway playing a vital role..”
Key Interview Insights
Create an experience that extends past what’s inside the payment plaform, but rather the platform itself
There needs to be innovations to allow for an easier accessibility and to promote reusability
Banking and Payment services go hand in hand, but right now have a bit of a rocky relationship
Just because its the common approach for long period of time, doesn’t mean it can’t be improved on
Transcations and bill payment without offers or reward may seems boring. Migrating to offers and discount packages can make a huge difference
Current trends of sustainability cater to the well-off and can be inaccessible to the lower class who have other matters to worry about

After the interviews and some secondary research, we found Banking Transcatons and Bill Payments to be major problem areas that both industries faced. We launched a survey to gain more insight on the topics of online Bank transfer, bill payment (such as cable tv, electricity), tickets, donating etc.

80% of our participants were 18 to 30 years old and 76% were male. We found that most of them do make bill payment and transactions through online payment platforms. Participants said they were most happy when payment service is accessible easily and fast , but a vast majority also cited Kuda as their most used site for the speed and convenience.  

Everyone has had some sort of online payment issues. User’s seemed frustrated by lack of transparency in the processes of buying, slow network, charges and bad user interface experience.

Research Conclusions

Based off the different interviews we conducted and the survey we sent out, we narrowed down our insights into 3 basic user needs we wanted to address

An good user interface, quick moving enterprise that’s hassle free for the user

An organized platform where you can quickly find and pay exactly for what you need

Eco-conscious and transparent business practices that helps not only the consumer but also the community, introducing incentives as users transacts.

from there, we created our Persona 's, user journey, and eventually developed out our final product, wogpay
