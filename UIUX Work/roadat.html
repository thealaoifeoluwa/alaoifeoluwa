Roadat

To support Roadat’s work of encouraging more diversity among the youth, Roadat partnered with Bigibrand to create a mobile app, with the hope that it will encourage greater diversity in the in the Motorcycle or Okada industry.

Role

UI/UX Designer

Tool

Figma, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop

Duration

10 weeks

Team

Self Directed, with guidance from mentor

The Problem Space

How can we create a mobile and web platform that will spike the youth's interest in the Motorcycle or Okada Industry, giving them easy access to movements and swift package deliveries, no matter their background?

Our Solution

Roadat plans to restructure this informal industry by providing tested, trusted and safe transportation for everyone..
